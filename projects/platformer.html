Platformer is the first game I made. I made it together with some friends, I did most of the game design and programming, we also used an asset pack for art. It's not a very special game, but I'm still proud of how succesful this first project was (over 5000 downloads on the Google Play Store).

I learned a lot of different skills by making this game, like how to work in a team, how to manage a published game, maintain it, update it, add new features/levels...
