Spaceship Survival




  • Start Date: August 2020
  • Last updated: August 2021
  • Team: Mostly me, got some help at the start and with sound
  • Genre(s): 2D, Arcade, Shooting, Bullet hell
  • Engine: Unity
  • Platform: Android


This is the second game I made in Unity, I also made the art myself. I implemented a lot more Google Play features in this game, like leaderboards and achievements.

The game is also a unique spin on the "dodge the missile" format, where the missiles (in this case enemy spaceships) can also shoot and have way more variety.

There are also a lot more extra features, like an enemy encyclopedia, a way to customize your spaceship cosmetically and functionally. There are also multiple worlds, enemies, powerups... I learned a lot about how many features a game can have that are not directly linked to gameplay, but players often expect them.
